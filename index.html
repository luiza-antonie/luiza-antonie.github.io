Luiza Antonie

      Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science at University of Guelph. I am working on developing algorithms for record linkage on census data. I am working with Dr. Kris Inwood and Historical Data Research Unit to build longitudinal data between 1852 and 1911 from Canadian Census data.
      I am interested in data mining (record linkage, classification, association rule mining), especially text and medical images mining, natural language processing and health informatics.
      I finished my PhD in the Computing Science Department at University of Alberta. I was part of the Database Research Group. At University of Alberta I worked with Dr. Osmar R. Zaïane and Dr. Robert Holte. My dissertation research was on associative classifiers.
