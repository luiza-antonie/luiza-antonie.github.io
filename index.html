I am an associate professor in the School of Computer Science, University of Guelph.
My research interests are in data mining techniques and methodology for interdisciplinary and applied research with a focus on record linkage, classification and data integration. Before joining the School of Computer Science, I was a postoctoral fellow with the Historical Data Research Unit. I finished my PhD in the Computing Science Department at University of Alberta. I was part of the Database Research Group. At University of Alberta I worked with Dr. Osmar R. Zaïane and Dr. Robert Holte. My dissertation research was on associative classifiers.
