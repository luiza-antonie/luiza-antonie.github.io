Luiza Antonie

I am an associate professor in the School of Computer Science, University of Guelph.

I am curretly on sabbatical visiting the Scottish Centre for Administrative Data Research at the University of Edinburgh.

My research interests are in data mining techniques and methodology for interdisciplinary and applied research with a focus on record linkage, classification and data integration.

Before joining the School of Computer Science, I was a postdoctoral fellow with the Historical Data Research Unit working with Dr. Kris Inwood. I finished my PhD in the Computing Science Department at University of Alberta where I worked with Dr. Osmar R. Zaïane and Dr. Robert Holte. I was part of the Database Research Group.
